About

I photograph the
places light forgets
to leave alone.

I'm Kiros Gan, a photographer drawn to strong geometry, decisive colour and quiet human moments inside enormous spaces. From the thermal blues of Yellowstone to vermilion temple eaves and rain-slick streets at night, every frame here was scouted, shot and edited by hand.

My approach is simple: wait for the light, find the line, and press the shutter at the exact second the scene resolves. The result is a portfolio that values patience over spectacle — though there's plenty of spectacle too.
